# 🎬 Movie Sales Analysis (IMDB Dataset)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes movie data from IMDB to uncover trends in box office revenue, genre popularity, audience ratings, and overall movie performance.

The analysis is performed using **Power BI**, focusing on transforming raw data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Identify top-performing genres, actors, and directors
* Analyze trends in movie revenue over time
* Understand audience preferences using ratings and votes
* Explore relationships between runtime, ratings, and revenue

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization

## 📈 Key Insights

* 📊 Box office revenue shows a steady upward trend over the years
* 🎬 A small number of blockbuster movies generate a large share of total revenue
* 🎭 Action and Adventure genres dominate revenue generation
* ⭐ Biography and History genres receive higher ratings but lower revenue
* 📉 Popular genres do not always guarantee higher audience satisfaction
* 👥 Higher audience engagement (votes) often correlates with higher revenue
* ⏱️ Movies with moderate runtime perform better commercially

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Focus on high-revenue genres like Action and Adventure
* Combine quality storytelling with mass appeal
* Invest in strong actors and directors
* Leverage franchise-based movie production
* Analyze audience feedback to improve performance
